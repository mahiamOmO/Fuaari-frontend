Our Story

Dorzi Tailoring was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, custom-tailored clothing that fits perfectly and makes you feel confident. Our journey began in a small workshop, where we combined traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to create timeless pieces.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name in the tailoring industry, serving clients from all walks of life. Whether it's a bespoke suit, a wedding dress, or a simple alteration, we pour our passion and expertise into every stitch.

Our Mission

At Dorzi, our mission is to redefine the art of tailoring by combining precision, creativity, and elegance. We believe that every piece of clothing tells a story, and we strive to make that story unforgettable.

We are committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly materials and practices wherever possible. Our goal is to create clothing that not only looks good but also feels good—for you and the planet.
